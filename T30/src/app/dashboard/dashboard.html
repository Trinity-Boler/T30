CleanPlay: Greener Gaming for a Sustainable Future

CleanPlay is a recent innovation launched in early 2025 that brings clean energy awareness directly to the gaming community. The platform allows players to offset the power their consoles use by subscribing to renewable energy sources such as wind and solar. It integrates with gaming systems like the PlayStation 5, rewarding users with exclusive in-game items, achievements, and digital collectibles for supporting clean power. This approach transforms gaming from being purely energy-consuming to being environmentally conscious and participatory. CleanPlay partners with energy providers and developers to verify renewable offsets, turning every gaming session into a contribution toward a cleaner energy grid. It represents a new intersection between entertainment technology and environmental responsibility, using gamification to inspire positive change within one of the largest digital industries.

Source: Sony Interactive: Enabling Environmental Action with the CleanPlay App (March 2025)

Technical Overview

The T30 project is built as a Single Page Application (SPA) using Angular for the frontend, Node.js and Express for the backend API, and MongoDB for the database. The site is hosted on NGINX for efficient static file serving and HTTPS configuration. The architecture follows a modern MERN-like stack but leverages Angular for improved modularity and scalability. Environment variables are managed with dotenv, and RESTful endpoints are used to fetch and display data dynamically from MongoDB.
